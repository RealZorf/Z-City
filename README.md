# 🚀 ARCXIV ZCITY

Z-City is a powerful **Garry’s Mod addon** that transforms the way you experience combat and movement 🎮⚡
It enhances **character damage systems** and **controls**, delivering a smoother, more dynamic, and immersive gameplay feel.

🔧 **What makes Z-City special?**

* 💥 Reworked and refined **damage mechanics**
* 🎯 Improved **player controls** for better responsiveness
* 🔫 Comes with its own **custom weapon base**
* 🗺️ Includes a unique **gamemode experience**
